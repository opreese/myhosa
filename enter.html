It's called rolling your logs.
If you are almost out of hours on your 70 and you want to keep working instead of doing a 34 hour reset, it may be possible that you will pickup enough hours from 8 days ago. In order to help your dispacther / broker assess this possibility then please enter your onduty hours for the last 7 days.
Note: Total Daily Onduty hours are each days combined hours (on duty + driving)

Hours entered in the 7 days ago box are the hours you will pick up tomorrow becuause by tomorrow those hours will be from 8 days ago and not included in the total onduty hours worked in the last 7 days.
